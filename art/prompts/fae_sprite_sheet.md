# Fae Sprite Sheet Prompt (4x3)

Create a 2D sprite sheet of a child adventurer named Fae in a cozy watercolor sticker style.

## Requirements
- Transparent background (TRUE alpha, no checkerboard)
- 4 directions: front, back, left, right
- 3 frames each: idle, step1, step2
- Consistent character scale and baseline alignment (feet on the same line)
- Soft watercolor shading, warm pastels, sepia/dark-brown outlines
- No text, no props outside the character

## Outfit
- Purple oversized hoodie
- Dark navy shorts
- Rainbow striped socks
- White sneakers with orange accents
- Glittery holographic backpack with star/heart stickers
- Messy brown hair with small ponytail + star hair clip

## Output
- Canvas: 768×1024
- Grid: 4 rows × 3 columns, equal cell sizes
- PNG with transparency
