# Arcade Backdrop Prompt

Create a cozy cutaway arcade interior diorama in soft watercolor style, angled top-down.

## Style
- Soft watercolor painting with diffuse warm lighting
- Warm pastels, low saturation, high brightness
- Dark brown/sepia outlines (no black)
- Storybook simplicity, clear readable shapes

## Scene content
- 6 arcade cabinets
- Claw machine
- Ticket counter
- Gumball machines
- Clear walkable lane from entrance to counter
- No characters, no floating text labels
