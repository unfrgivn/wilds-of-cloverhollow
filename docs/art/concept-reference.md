# Concept Art Reference Guide

This document distills the aesthetic direction from concept art in `docs/art/concepts/`. Use it to guide asset creation. You do not need to copy concepts exactly, but they establish the visual language.

## Character Design

### Core Principles
- **Proportions**: Chibi/super-deformed style (1:1.5 to 1:2 head-to-body ratio).
- **Readability**: Simple, clear silhouettes that read well at 16×16 pixel scale.
- **Age range**: Characters look 8–12 years old (target audience).
- **Vibe**: Cozy, wholesome, adventurous.

### Protagonist (Fae)
Reference: `concepts/characters/character_concept_sheet_fae.png`

- **Hair**: Brown messy bun with star-shaped hair clip.
- **Face**: Fair skin, rosy cheeks/nose, simple black dot eyes.
- **Outfit**: Muted purple oversized hoodie, navy shorts, white+orange sneakers.
- **Accessories**: Rainbow stripe socks, holographic backpack with sticker decorations.
- **Alternate costumes**: See `fae_alternate_costumes.png` for seasonal/event variants.

### Supporting Characters
Reference: `concepts/characters/sue_concept_sheet.png`, `jordan_concept_sheet.png`

- Each party member has distinct silhouette and color palette.
- Maintain consistent proportions across all characters.
- Accessories and clothing reflect personality.

### NPCs
Reference: `concepts/npcs/`

- **Town NPCs**: Friendly adults with occupational signifiers (aprons, tools, etc.).
- **School Staff**: Teacher and principal designs in `school_staff.png`.
- **Critters**: Non-scary animal NPCs in `npc_critters.png`.
- **Family**: Fae's family members in `fae_family.png`.
- **Pets**: Companion designs like `maddie_kitten.png`.
- **Antagonist elements**: `chaos_raccoon.png` for mischief-themed characters.

## Environment Design

### Cloverhollow Town
Reference: `concepts/environments/town_center_plaza.png`

- **Style**: Soft painterly watercolor aesthetic with thin brown outlines.
- **Ground**: Warm sunny pastels (creamy yellows, beiges, tans for cobblestone/paths).
- **Buildings**: Warm browns for wood, terracotta red roofs, cream/white walls.
- **Awnings**: Striped patterns in complementary colors (yellow/orange, blue/white, green/white).
- **Nature**: Muted greens for grass, bushes, flower accents.
- **Fixtures**: Wrought-iron lampposts with hanging flower baskets, wooden benches.
- **Vibe**: Cozy, cheerful, safe European village hub.

### Town Shops
Reference: `concepts/props/town_shops.png`

- Each shop has distinct facade and signage.
- Display windows show merchandise.
- Outdoor elements (planters, A-frame signs, barrels).

### Interior Spaces

#### Arcade
Reference: `concepts/environments/arcade_interior.png`, `concepts/props/arcade_props.png`

- Colorful cabinet machines.
- Neon accent lighting (as colored glows, not garish).
- Prize counter with plushies.
- Checkered or patterned floors.

#### School
Reference: `concepts/environments/school_interior.png`

- Desks in rows, chalkboard, bulletin boards.
- Lockers in hallways.
- Bright, clean, institutional but friendly.

#### Hero's Bedroom
Reference: `concepts/environments/hero_house_bedroom.png`

- Personal and lived-in (posters, toys, books).
- Cozy bed with colorful bedding.
- Desk with lamp and craft supplies.

#### Clubhouse
Reference: `concepts/environments/clubhouse_exterior.png`

- Treehouse aesthetic.
- Hand-built, whimsical, "kids only" feel.
- Rope ladder, wooden planks, bunting/flags.

### Outdoor Areas

#### Park/Meadow
Reference: `concepts/environments/meadow_park.png`, `concepts/props/park_props.png`

- Rolling grass, wildflowers, scattered trees.
- Benches, trash cans, picnic tables.
- Pond with bridge, stepping stones.

#### Beach
Reference: `concepts/environments/beach_cove.png`

- Soft sand tones, blue-green water.
- Shells, starfish, driftwood props.
- Beach umbrellas, towels.

#### Mountain
Reference: `concepts/environments/pinecone_pass.png`

- Pine trees, rocky outcrops.
- Cooler color palette (blue-greens, grays).
- Snow caps on distant peaks.

## Props and Items

### Tools
Reference: `concepts/props/tools.png`, `concepts/props/blacklight_lantern.png`

- **Lantern**: Key exploration tool with magical glow.
- Tools should look kid-friendly and slightly oversized for readability.

### Food Items
Reference: `concepts/props/food_items.png`

- Colorful, appetizing, slightly stylized.
- Healing items, shop inventory, quest rewards.

### Collectibles
Reference: `concepts/collectibles/candy_gems.png`, `concepts/collectibles/stickers.png`

- **Candy Gems**: Currency/collectibles with gemstone shapes.
- **Stickers**: Reward/achievement system items.

### Furniture and Decor
Reference: `concepts/props/cardboard_fort.png`

- Crafted, handmade aesthetic for kid spaces.
- Mix of proper furniture and improvised items.

## UI Design

Reference: `concepts/ui/ui_kit_sticker_style_for_a_cozy_.png`

### Core Aesthetic: "Cozy Sticker"
- **Base**: Cream/off-white paper-craft look.
- **Borders**: Thick off-white die-cut sticker borders.
- **Outlines**: Thin dark brown hand-drawn lines.
- **Shadows**: Soft drop shadows for depth.
- **Background**: Crumpled cream paper texture (subtle).

### Typography
- Rounded sans-serif, hand-written feel.
- All uppercase for headers/buttons.
- Dark brown text color.

### Colors
- **Primary background**: Cream/off-white.
- **Text**: Dark brown.
- **Accents**: Soft pastels.
  - Energy/health: Soft green with stripes.
  - Currency/gems: Lavender.
  - Prompts/actions: Muted cyan.

### Components
- **Bars**: Rounded ends, leaf/nature icons.
- **Counters**: Circular with bold numbers.
- **Buttons**: Rounded rectangles with thick borders.
- **Speech bubbles**: Stitch effects on edges.
- **Interaction prompts**: Hand-drawn arrow indicators.

## VFX

Reference: `concepts/vfx/particles.png`

- Simple particle effects for magic, healing, attacks.
- Soft, non-threatening visual effects.
- Color-coded by effect type.

## Pixel Art Translation

When translating concepts to pixel art:

1. **Simplify shapes**: Reduce detail to what reads at 16×16 or 32×32.
2. **Limit palette**: Use biome + global palette only.
3. **Maintain proportions**: Keep chibi ratios consistent.
4. **Preserve silhouettes**: Character shapes must be recognizable.
5. **Clean outlines**: Single-pixel dark outlines preferred.
6. **Limited shading**: 2–3 shade bands per material maximum.
