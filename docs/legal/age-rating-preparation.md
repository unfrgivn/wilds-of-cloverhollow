## ESRB Equivalent Rating

**E (Everyone)** - Mild Fantasy Violence

The game would qualify for an E rating under ESRB guidelines due to:
- Mild fantasy violence in turn-based combat
- Comic mischief from "villain" character
- No other concerning content
