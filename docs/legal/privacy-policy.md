# Privacy Policy

**Wilds of Cloverhollow**

**Last Updated:** January 26, 2026

**Effective Date:** January 26, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Clover Studios ("we", "our", or "us") respects the privacy of users ("you", "user") of our mobile game Wilds of Cloverhollow (the "Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Game.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Save Game Data**: Game progress, inventory, and story flags stored locally on your device.
- **Settings Preferences**: Audio volume, text size, and accessibility options.

### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for advertising purposes
- Contact lists or photos
- Payment information (the Game is free with no in-app purchases)

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Information
Currently, we do not collect any automatic analytics data. The analytics and crash reporting systems in the Game are stubs for potential future use and do not transmit any data externally.

## 3. How We Use Information

All information is stored locally on your device and is used solely to:
- Save and restore your game progress
- Remember your accessibility and audio preferences
- Track achievements and collection progress within the Game

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Wilds of Cloverhollow is designed for children ages 8-12 and their families. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations:

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
- We do not require account creation or login.
- All data remains on the device and is never transmitted to our servers.
- Parents can delete all game data by uninstalling the app.

## 6. Data Storage and Security

- All game data is stored locally on your device using Godot Engine's secure user data directory.
- Save files are stored in JSON format in the app's sandboxed storage.
- We do not have access to your local save data.

## 7. Third-Party Services

The Game does not currently integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. If this changes in future updates, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

## 8. Cloud Saves (Future Feature)

If cloud save functionality is implemented in a future update:
- Cloud saves will be opt-in only.
- Data will be encrypted in transit and at rest.
- You will be able to delete cloud data at any time.
- This Privacy Policy will be updated before the feature launches.

## 9. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View your save data (stored locally as JSON files).
- **Delete**: Uninstall the app to remove all local data.
- **Opt-out**: Not use any optional features that may collect data in the future.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
